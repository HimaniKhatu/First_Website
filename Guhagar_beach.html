Guhagar beach

♦About Guhagar Beach:

Guhagar Beach is a tranquil and scenic coastal stretch located in the Ratnagiri district of Maharashtra along the pristine Konkan coast. Known for its clean white sand, calm turquoise waters, and backdrop of swaying coconut palms, the beach stretches over 6 kilometers, offering an unspoiled and peaceful retreat far from crowded tourist destinations. Guhagar is ideal for leisurely walks, swimming, or simply enjoying a quiet sunset by the sea. Its charm is enhanced by nearby attractions like the ancient Vyadeshwar and Durga Devi temples, as well as the picturesque Anjanvel Lighthouse. With its natural beauty and laid-back vibe, Guhagar Beach is a perfect getaway for nature lovers, families, and anyone seeking a serene beach experience in Maharashtra.

Durga Devi Temple

♦About Durga Devi Temple:

Durga Devi Temple, Guhagar is a serene and ancient Hindu temple located in the coastal town of Guhagar, in the Ratnagiri district of Maharashtra. This temple is dedicated to Goddess Durga, revered as the family deity (Kuladevata) by many Chitpavan Brahmin families in the Konkan region. Nestled amidst lush greenery and traditional Konkani surroundings, the temple offers a peaceful and spiritual atmosphere.The temple is built in the traditional Panchayatan style, with the main shrine devoted to Goddess Durga, and smaller shrines around it dedicated to Lord Ganesh, Lord Shiva, Lord Vishnu, and Surya (Sun God). According to local legends, Goddess Durga is believed to have protected the village from evil forces, and a precious pearl from her ornament said to be found near a tree by the sea is preserved in the temple to this day.
